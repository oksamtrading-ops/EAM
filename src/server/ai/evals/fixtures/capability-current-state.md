# Current-State Review — Acme Retail

## Customer Management

Acme runs a customer management function across its omnichannel estate. This
is structured around three main sub-capabilities:

- **Lead Management**: capture inbound leads from the website, trade shows,
  and partner channels, qualify them, and route to the sales team.
- **Opportunity Management**: pipeline management, forecast, and
  account-plan tracking for the enterprise sales organisation.
- **Customer Service**: case intake, tier-1 triage, and escalation to
  product specialists.

## Marketing

Marketing owns demand generation and campaign execution. Today it's
fragmented across two tools; consolidation is a Q3 initiative.

- **Campaign Execution**: email, paid digital, and event marketing.
- **Brand Management**: creative asset library and brand-guideline
  enforcement across regional teams.

## Finance & Accounting

The F&A function covers the full record-to-report lifecycle.

- **General Ledger**: core GL and chart-of-accounts, consolidated in
  NetSuite.
- **Accounts Payable**: invoice capture and approvals, still partly manual.

---

## Applications

- **Salesforce Sales Cloud** — primary CRM, owned by Revenue Ops.
  Active, integrated with NetSuite.
- **NetSuite ERP** — core financials, owned by Finance. Active.
- **Legacy Mainframe Billing** — IBM mainframe, being sunset (EOL 2027).
  Owned by IT, considered a critical technology risk.

## Known risks

- **Mainframe EOL 2027** — billing system has no replacement plan;
  vendor support ends 2027.
- **Dual BI overlap** — both Tableau and Power BI are in active use with
  duplicate reporting.
